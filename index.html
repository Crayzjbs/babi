Hello love...

Hello love, it has been a hard month for us. We had our biggest fight and we almost lost it all. I am sorry that it took other people to tell me that my ways were putting pressure on you. I am ashamed but I am grateful to be surrounded by friends who want us to be strong.

I now understand the hard truth. Never reason ang attachments para mag stay lang ka dira and it is a sign na naga point sa unsay need nimo i work on, and that is yung abandonment issues ko. I was afraid na ang space kay threat, when in fact it is the most important thing we should have, to always be calm and collected, to never say words that we do not mean, to be secure sa self nato.

I forgot the importance of individuality, that we have our own dreams, our own ways, and our own beliefs. I have come to my senses that I was too needy, na di ako secure. Yes I still feel anxious kapag may space tayo but ever since I started to love myself again, naging engaged nasad ko sa gym, mas na lingaw kog isip og mga new hobbies to try. And having that means dili sad tika pabayaan, it just means that we can still enjoy ourselves through the hobbies we both enjoy, through the things that we value, and through loving each other, but also through the things that make us happy personally, that remind us of who we are.

I also learned the value of pausing, love. To be calm and collected always, to feel emotions but to control them, because we are not ourselves when we are angry or nag away. And it was selfish of me to say na need i fix agad ang problems, need mag bigay ng solutions, need agad, need now, need urgent, because being that needy is disrespecting your own battles.

I still do appreciate assurance but that should first come from within me and not be required from you. I would still love to hear it but it will never be a reason to pressure someone I love.

I love you. I have learned my lessons and I apologize, sayo and sa self ko, kay mami. I will work on this.

I love you love ko, I really do. So take these lilies as my commitment to our rebirth, my devotion sayo. May we grow together and be strong together. I love you so much.

— Kin
